## Skin Care Tips for Dubai's Climate

Dubai's heat, humidity, and air conditioning can stress your skin. Follow these tips:

1. **Cleanse twice daily** with a gentle, pH-balanced cleanser
2. **Hydrate with lightweight moisturizer** suitable for your skin type
3. **Use SPF 50+ daily** — even on cloudy days
4. **Exfoliate 1-2 times per week** to remove dead skin cells
5. **Get professional facials monthly** for deeper care
