## What Is Root Canal Treatment?

Root canal treatment removes infected or damaged pulp from inside your tooth. It is the best way to save a tooth that would otherwise need extraction.

## Signs You May Need a Root Canal

- Persistent tooth pain, especially when chewing
- Sensitivity to hot or cold that lingers
- Swollen or tender gums near a specific tooth
- Darkening or discoloration of a tooth
- A cracked or chipped tooth
