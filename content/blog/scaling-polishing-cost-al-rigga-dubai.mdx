## Why Is Professional Scaling Important?

Even with regular brushing and flossing, plaque hardens into tartar that only a professional can remove. Scaling and polishing every 6 months:

- Prevents gum disease and gingivitis
- Removes surface stains for brighter teeth
- Eliminates bad breath caused by bacteria
- Allows early detection of dental issues

## What Happens During Scaling and Polishing

1. **Oral examination:** Check for cavities, gum disease, and other issues
2. **Scaling:** Ultrasonic and hand instruments remove tartar above and below the gum line
3. **Polishing:** Prophy paste smoothens tooth surfaces
4. **Fluoride (optional):** Strengthens enamel and prevents decay
5. **Aftercare advice:** Brushing techniques and product recommendations

## How Often Should You Get Scaling Done?

- **Every 6 months:** For most people with good oral health
- **Every 3-4 months:** If you have gum disease or smoke
- **Every 6-12 months:** If you have excellent oral hygiene
