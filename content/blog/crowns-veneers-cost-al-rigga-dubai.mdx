## What's the Difference Between Crowns and Veneers?

**Veneers** are thin shells (0.3-0.5mm) bonded to the front surface of teeth. They are primarily cosmetic.

**Crowns** cover the entire tooth and provide both aesthetic and structural restoration.

## When to Choose Veneers

Veneers are ideal for:

- Closing small gaps between teeth
- Covering discolored or stained teeth
- Fixing chipped or worn teeth
- Reshaping slightly misaligned teeth
- Creating a uniform, aesthetic smile

## When to Choose Crowns

Crowns are recommended for:

- Heavily filled or weakened teeth
- Teeth after root canal treatment
- Significant cracks or fractures
- Covering dental implants
- Restoring severe decay
